Doc Edge is back for its 13th year, bringing us Aucklanders all the feels with a variety of international documentaries. There’s 74 stunning films to catch, and no doubt you’ll find one perfectly suited to you as they cover everything from pop culture, politics to our very own Pacific. Opening with Antone’s Arc, this flick takes an up-close and personal look at the crisis faced by the low-lying atoll nation of Kiribati. Highlights include award winners Of Fathers and Sons, The Other Side of Everything and The Long Season, as well as the World Premiere of the Italian documentary Postcards from India—A Busker’s Adventure. Doc Edge is back for its 13th year, bringing us Aucklanders all the feels with a variety of er’s Adventure.
